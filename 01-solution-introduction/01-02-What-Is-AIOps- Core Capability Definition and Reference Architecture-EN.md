## Five Core Competencies

**Five core competencies**: from reactive response to intelligent operations.

The upgrade of the operations paradigm depends on the deep integration of the **brain (AI)** and the **nerve tentacles (automation)**: see clearly, interact naturally, then triage, analyze, and execute with trust, all within enterprise-grade security boundaries.

---

## ① Interaction

> *"If you can talk, you can operate."*

- Use enterprise chat tools as the **unified entry point** to lower the barrier to adoption.
- Reduce complex operations to **natural language interaction** and deliver ChatOps.

**Goal**: Enable L1 staff to complete tasks that previously required L3-level skills.

---

## ② Observability

> *"You can only manage what you can see."*

- Build an **end-to-end observability platform** first, then connect the four signal sources.
- Unify **Metrics / Logs / Traces / Events** to eliminate observability blind spots.

**Goal**: Achieve **full-stack observability** from underlying hardware to the application layer.

---

## ③ Triage & Execution

> *"Instant forensics → intelligent cleansing → trusted execution"*

- Use **EDA real-time forensics** to preserve the failure "black box" and avoid losing transient incident context.
- Use **AAP intelligent cleansing** to turn noisy raw alerts into structured, high-quality AI decision assets.

**Goal**: Support **Agentic AI** to make high-quality decisions in complex scenarios and **execute safely and reliably**.

---

## ④ AI Analysis

> *"Growing smarter with the business"*

- **RAG** injects deterministic domain knowledge and expert experience into AI.
- **Skills** give automated execution adaptive, evolutionary capabilities.

**Goal**: AI collaborative analysis is no longer a static system, but a digital asset that **keeps improving as the business grows**.

---

## ⑤ Security & Compliance

> *"Lock autonomy inside security boundaries"*

- **Policy Enforcement** delivers policy-driven trusted execution and correction.
- **Agent security domains** + **MCP RBAC** + **prompt injection protection** provide layered safeguards for autonomous AI actions.

**Goal**: Keep AI autonomy **within enterprise security boundaries** for highly trusted, low-risk intelligent operations.
