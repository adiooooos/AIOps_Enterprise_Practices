# Why AIOps - Traditional Operations Pain Points & AI Core Value

> **Status**: 20260603 Updated  
> **Series**: AIOps DEMO Center Deploy & Setup Step-by-Step Guide  


## Core Insight

The operations paradigm is shifting from **reactive response** to **proactive governance**. The core value of an AI Agent is not to replace operations teams, but to fill the gaps in human monitoring capacity, repetitive execution, knowledge retention, and cross-system correlation analysis.

## Traditional Pain Points & AI Value

| Dimension | Traditional Challenges | AI Value Proposition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Silos** | Environments are closed and data is scattered. A single troubleshooting case often requires multiple rounds of coordination across teams. | The Agent aggregates service, resource, log, and database status in seconds, then provides a high-quality initial assessment. |
| **Toil** | Health checks, restarts, certificate validation, and other manual tasks consume 70%+ of operations capacity. | AI takes over repetitive L1/L2 tasks and executes them consistently 24x7, freeing human capacity. |
| **Brain Drain** | Operational knowledge is lost when people move roles or leave. SOPs, scripts, and cases lack a sustainable retention mechanism. | RAG turns expert knowledge into an organizational "digital brain" for long-term reuse and continuity. |
| **Forensics** | Transient failures such as Panic or Hung events may disappear after a restart, leaving RCA without a complete evidence chain. | EDA captures stack traces and SOS data within milliseconds, keeping the evidence chain complete and traceable. |
| **Capacity** | Metrics are fragmented and rarely monitored continuously. Small risks can silently grow into major incidents. | Cross-platform correlation analysis automatically identifies capacity risks and provides FinOps optimization recommendations. |
| **Compliance** | Configuration drift and vulnerabilities are difficult to detect in real time. Manual audits are delayed and incomplete. | Policy Enforcement audits and corrects drift in real time, keeping systems continuously aligned with the expected state. |
